## Goal for this iteration

PLAN's "next ordinary iteration" (M6.3.1) is the out-of-tree
one-shot needing live Fly + Cloudflare tokens — not runnable
inside autodev. Iter 53's notes explicitly flagged the next
small, well-defined cleanup: the two redirect branches in
`hooks.server.ts` (unauth→`/`, authed-on-`/`→`/editor`) had no
direct unit coverage. Extracting them into a pure
`routeRedirect({session, method, pathname}) → location | null`
helper makes both branches testable without spinning up
SvelteKit, and removes the only behaviour-bearing logic from a
file that otherwise just wires together I/O.

## Approach

- New `apps/web/src/lib/server/routeRedirect.ts`. Pure: no
  imports beyond `ResolvedSession`. Exports the policy constants
  (`SIGNED_OUT_PATH`, `SIGNED_IN_HOME`, `PROTECTED_PREFIXES`,
  `SIGN_IN_PAGE_PATHS`) so the hook stays a one-liner consumer
  and the test pins the configured set.
- `hooks.server.ts`: drop the two inline conditionals and the
  hard-coded constants; replace with a single `routeRedirect(...)`
  call. `clearCookie` handling unchanged.
- New `apps/web/test/routeRedirect.test.mjs` covering both
  redirect branches, the GET-only filter on `/`, prefix-match on
  `/editor/abc`, and the four no-redirect cases (authed visiting
  protected, unauth on `/`, unauth on unprotected, authed on
  unprotected). Plus an exported-constants sanity check so a
  silent edit to `PROTECTED_PREFIXES` etc. trips the test.
- Registered in `tests_normal/cases/test_node_suites.py`.
