  confirmed root cause — `apps/sidecar/src/server.ts:243`
  `scheduleCompile` gates only on a pending timer, not on an
  in-flight compile. A doc-update during a long round

  state machine fixes this.

  access today. Chose the **skeleton/placeholder** option over
  SSR to avoid wiring control-plane → blob-store / sidecar HTTP.
