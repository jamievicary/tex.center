## Things not done (intentional)

PLAN's "if feasible" stretch goal was to extend the probe to send a
Yjs sync-step-1 frame after upgrade and assert the upstream doesn't
502 / cgroup-kill mid-stream. Skipped this iteration for two
reasons:

1. The probe script's `http.request` upgrade path destroys the
   socket immediately on the `upgrade` event (line 62), so adding a
   payload-bearing extension is a real edit, not a trivial
   tweak — closer to writing a small WS client (frame masking,
   sync-step-1 Yjs encoding) than to extending a probe.
2. The iteration has already consumed ~9 min of wallclock on the CD
   wait + two cold-start probes. Bundling the WS-client work risks
   tipping over 45 min, and the cleaner alternative is to land it
   in iter 156 alongside the M8.pw.4 activation work (which exercises
   the same code paths automatically).
