  (cmContentReadyMs=857 numbers are accurate for that path).

  `stopped` (sidecar idle-handler fallback `exit(0)` lands a
  Machine in `stopped`, not `suspended`).

  zero per-project Machines were in `suspended`. Either all
  recently touched, or the self-suspend handler isn't reliably
  landing in `suspended`.

  promoted: add a sibling case driving `POST /machines/{id}/stop`
  with the same 1000 ms budget. Expected RED.
