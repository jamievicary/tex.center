## Goal for this iteration

Iteration 20 is a refactor iteration (N % 10 == 0). Survey
identified one clear high-leverage dedup target: the `SpawnFn`
test seam was hand-rolled identically in three files
(`compiler/featureDetect.ts`, `compiler/supertexOnce.ts`,
`compiler/supertexWatch.ts`), and both supertex-backed compilers
independently computed the conventional layout
`<workDir>/out/{shipouts, <basename>.pdf}`. Consolidate both into
a small `compiler/supertexShared.ts`.

Other large files (`server.ts` 337 lines, `supertexWatch.ts` 299
lines) are coherent as-is — `server.ts` is the WS entry point
where the duplication is between projects, not within the file;
`supertexWatch.ts` is a single state machine. Not worth churning.

## Approach

New `apps/sidecar/src/compiler/supertexShared.ts` exporting:

- `SpawnFn` — the shared spawn-function signature.
- `defaultSpawnFn` — `nodeSpawn as SpawnFn`, the default.
- `supertexPaths(workDir, sourceName)` →
  `{ outDir, shipoutsPath, pdfPath }`.

Update the three compiler files to import from it; delete their
local copies and the now-unused `node:child_process` /
`node:path` symbols.
