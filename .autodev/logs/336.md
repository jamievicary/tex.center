## Goal for this iteration

Iter 335 added per-phase `[verifyLiveGt9...] elapsedMs=… phase=…`
diagnostic logs throughout GT-9 and raised its per-test wall to 8 min
specifically so a mid-flight failure would name its phase. The iter-335
gold pass then surfaced a different problem: the outer
`tests_gold/cases/test_playwright.py` process timed out at its own
900-s `subprocess.run(..., timeout=900)` ceiling, and because that call
uses `capture_output=True`, the resulting `TimeoutExpired` was raised
*before* the lines that flush `result.stdout` / `result.stderr` —
silently dropping the entire Playwright list-reporter output along
with every GT-9 phase marker. Net effect: the iter-335 diagnostic
investment produced zero diagnostic data, and iter 335's gold log
shows only `TimeoutExpired: ... timed out after 900 seconds` with no
indication of which spec was stuck.

This iteration restores diagnostic visibility on outer timeout:

1. Catch `subprocess.TimeoutExpired` in `test_playwright.py`. The
   exception's `.stdout` / `.stderr` attributes carry whatever the
   child captured before the wall fired (this is a documented
   `subprocess` contract when `capture_output=True`). Dump them on
   our stdout/stderr exactly as the success path does, then raise an
   `AssertionError` naming the elapsed timeout. Per-spec ✓ / ✘ lines
   and console.logs (specifically GT-9's phase markers) reach the
   iteration log even on suite-level wall fire.
2. Raise the outer wall from 900 s → 1200 s. globalSetup's own
   240-s budget plus ~25 live specs + local specs + GT-9's full
   8-min worst case sat right at the 900 s edge; 20 minutes gives
   one slow spec headroom without masking a real regression.

No behavioural change to any spec or to the normal suite.

## Actions

- `tests_gold/cases/test_playwright.py`:
  - Wrap the `subprocess.run([...], timeout=…)` call in
    `try / except subprocess.TimeoutExpired`.
  - In the handler: write `exc.stdout` / `exc.stderr` (if present)
    to `sys.stdout` / `sys.stderr`, flush, then
    `raise AssertionError(...) from exc`. The message records the
    timeout in seconds and points the reader at the captured output
    above for the "last logged phase" diagnostic.
  - Bump `timeout=900` → `timeout=1200` with an inline comment
    naming the worst-case sum (globalSetup ~240 s + ~25 live
    specs + local specs + GT-9 worst case 480 s).
