## Goal for this iteration

Periodic refactor (`N % 10 == 0`). Highest-leverage target: the blob
persistence policy in `apps/sidecar/src/server.ts`. It has grown
across iters 24, 25, 28, 29 into a sprawl of `ProjectState` fields
(`persistedSource`, `canPersist`, `hydrated`), an inline hydration
IIFE inside `getProject`, a duplicate persistence branch in
`runCompile`, and `defaultBlobStoreFromEnv` sitting at the bottom of
the file. Centralising it gives the `canPersist` invariant a local
owner (the original bug in iter 29 was about exactly which gates
applied where) and a clear seat for multi-file work.

## Approach

1. New `apps/sidecar/src/persistence.ts` exposing
   `createProjectPersistence({ blobStore, projectId, text, log })`
   returning `{ awaitHydrated, maybePersist }`.
   - When `blobStore` is undefined, both methods are no-ops.
   - When set, kicks off hydration immediately, sets a closed-over
     `canPersist` flag only on the no-throw path, and the
     `maybePersist(source)` gate is `canPersist && source !==
     persistedSource`.
2. Move `mainTexKey` and `defaultBlobStoreFromEnv` to the same
   module — they belong with the persistence policy.
3. `server.ts`: drop the three `ProjectState` fields, replace with
   one `persistence: ProjectPersistence`. Hydration IIFE and
   persistence branch become single calls.
