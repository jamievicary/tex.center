its tests. The plan bullet imagines wiring it into the once-compiler
path, but reading both compilers makes clear neither needs it:

- `SupertexOnceCompiler` returns after the child process exits, by

  as the canonical stability signal

streaming compile path that never materialised. It has zero
non-test callers (`grep awaitPdfStable` confirms). Per CLAUDE.md
"don't design for hypothetical future requirements" — delete it.
If a future compile shape ever needs filesystem-stability polling,
the iter-41 implementation is recoverable from git.
